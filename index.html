Trust

How we hold your voice.

Four principles

You enroll yourself.

You upload your own reference clips. We never scrape your voice from the web to build your fingerprint.

No raw audio stored.

We extract a mathematical fingerprint and discard the audio. Fingerprints are encrypted and purge at 30 days unless you keep them active.

Matches are leads.

Every alert is yours to confirm or dismiss. The system never files a takedown on your behalf without explicit confirmation.

You own the evidence.

Each match exports as a packet — timestamped, source-linked, waveform-overlaid — ready for legal, platform appeals, or your agency.

Why now

The case for watching.

Three reasons

If your voice is your work, a convincing clone is a business problem — not a future one.

  1. 01

    You find out last.

    Most creators learn their voice has been cloned from a listener or a fan — weeks after a clone is already in circulation.

  2. 02

    Takedowns weren't built for audio.

    Platform reporting flows were designed for text impersonation. Audio sits in a slower lane, and the clock runs while you wait.

  3. 03

    Late detection is a brand event.

    A clone in a scam, a fake endorsement, a fabricated interview — catching it in 24 hours is a different story than catching it in four weeks.
