How it works

Start narrow, prove the signal, then expand.

01

Verify your voice

Add a small set of approved voice samples so the system has a consented baseline for comparison.

02

Monitor public audio

Begin with high-signal sources like public videos, podcasts, and creator platforms instead of claiming to scan everything.

03

Review suspicious matches

See the source, timestamp, confidence level, and context before deciding whether it is misuse, parody, licensed use, or noise.

04

Prepare evidence

Export a clean report for takedown requests, platform appeals, managers, agencies, or legal review.

Trust model

The product only works if people trust it with their voice.

Consent-first samples

Voice references should be explicitly uploaded, labeled, and removable by the owner.

Privacy by default

Short retention windows, encryption, and clear deletion controls need to be core features.

Human review

Matches should be treated as leads, not accusations. False positives are part of the workflow.

Useful outcomes

Detection should lead to reports, takedown templates, and clean evidence collection.
